Why Study with Us.

1. Guaranteed place

Get a guaranteed place at the University of Sheffield (dependent on achieving the required grades) and progress directly to your second year. Our programme is certified by the University and quality assured by NCUK to ensure you get the most out of your education.

2. Join an elite university

The University of Sheffield is ranked among the World Top 100 (QS World University Rankings 2021) and is a member of the prestigious Russell Group of UK universities. Studying our International Year One gives you direct access to studying at this elite university.

3. An extraordinary place to study

Sheffield is one of the UK’s most popular places for students to go to university. The city is large enough to offer a great range of restaurants, shopping, theatre, cinemas, bars and culture; yet small enough to find your way around quickly and feel part of the community.

4. Graduate in three years

By spending the first year of your degree in the supportive environment of the International College – you’ll progress directly to the second year of your degree to ensure you graduate in the three years it takes a direct entry student.
